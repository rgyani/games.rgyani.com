Survive the wild, master the elements, solve puzzles, scale cliffs, swing ropes, leap ledges, outsmart animals, and find your way home.

Wild Horizon: Edge of Survival is an adventure game set amidst the perilous wilderness. As the lone survivor of a devastating plane crash, you must navigate a rugged, rocky landscape, outwit fierce predators, and solve challenging puzzles to survive. With only the terrain around you and your instincts to rely on, each step forward brings you closer to safety—or danger.

Key Features:

  • Immersive Third-Person Exploration: Explore a breathtaking, untamed world filled with jagged cliffs, steep slopes, and awe-inspiring vistas. The environment is as beautiful as it is deadly, drawing you into the heart of nature's raw power.
  • Dynamic Movement and Challenges: Master fluid parkour mechanics as you climb cliffs, leap across narrow gaps, and swing over deep chasms. Every move demands precision and drains your stamina, making timing and planning essential for survival.
  • Intricate Puzzle Mechanics: Unlock vital items to aid your journey by solving puzzles that test your observation and logic.
  • Wildlife Encounters: Outsmart dangerous animals to escape unscathed, using your surroundings and clever tactics instead of brute force.

The Story

You awaken in the aftermath of a catastrophic plane crash, alone and isolated from the rest of the world. And since its a game, you don't have your smartphone with you.Your one hope lies in reaching the wreckage of the aircraft to repair the radio and call for help. However, your path is fraught with natural obstacles—treacherous rocks, slick slopes, and dizzying cliffs make even the simplest tasks a deadly endeavor.

This is how most survival games begin. But in Wild Horizon, you will NOT be gathering stones and branches, chopping down trees, or building cozy cabins and crafting coffee tables. Let's be real—you're not here to start a new life in the wild! Your goal is to get back home... because there are way easier games waiting.

!

Precision and Stamina: Your Keys to Survival

Your journey relies purely on your skills, timing, and quick thinking. Every parkour leap, every climb, every jump between ledges, and each carefully timed rope swing drains your stamina. You'll need to manage your stamina wisely—lose focus, and you risk slipping, plummeting into the depths below.

!

Nature's Ferocious Hunters

But the wilderness isn't just about cliffs and heights. Predatory animals lurk here, too. Unlike other games, blasting your way through isn't an option. Let's face it—facing a bloodthirsty wolf or a 400-pound grizzly with makeshift gear isn't going to end well...for you. These animals are fierce hunters, but you've got something they don't: wits. Outsmarting them is your best shot.

!

!

!

Think, Solve, Survive

Along the journey, you'll find locked suitcases that could contain life-saving items—but opening them isn't as simple as turning a key. You'll need keen observation and logical deduction to open them, while a wrong combination will set you back, forcing you to reassess and try again.

!

Your survival depends on navigating a world that is as unforgiving as it is beautiful. Will you be able to outlast the wilderness and call for help, or will the Wild Horizon be your final view?
